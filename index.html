Navigation Tips

  • Download or open the contract
  • Use the "find" feature by pressing “CTRL” and the “F” key at the same time on a PC or Command (or Cmd) ⌘ and and the “F” key at the same time on a Mac - a search bar will populate
  • In the search bar type in key words that you are looking for, such as “PTO” or “Salary Grade”
  • The search bar will generate a list of areas throughout the contract with those keywords, from there you are able to select the sections that you would like to read
  • If you are unsure of the section that you are wanting to read simply hit the up or down keys to move to the next highlighted key word
